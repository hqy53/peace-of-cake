Peace Of Cake

Peace Of Cake is Toronto's one stop destination for delightful desserts. We believe in delivering a variety of authentic desserts from a global perspective. It might be the last course on the menu, but we all know we'd prefer it first!

BESTSELLERS

GULAAB JAMUN

Ever heard of something like a ball of happiness? Whoever said that must be thinking of Gulaab Jamuns

Deeply fried and loved, Gulaab Jamuns, a famous mithai amidst the Indian cuisine, are made up of milk powder & khoya, later garnished with nuts.

BEIGNETS

Warm beignets filled with gooey raspberry filling topped with powdered sugar

Beignets, fluffy french fritter doughnuts are square-cut in shape and made up of deep-fried & sweetened pâte à choux and garnished with granulated sugar or syrup of choice.

TIRAMISU

Creamy Tiramisu expresso-soaked ladyfingers surrounded by lightly sweetened whipped cream and rich mascarpone

A layered Venetian delight made up with savoiardi, egg yolks, mascarpone, coffee and liquor of choice is definitely going to "cheer you up!" Tiramisu is a perfect grandmother's recipe which is best served cold with hot chocolate or espresso!
